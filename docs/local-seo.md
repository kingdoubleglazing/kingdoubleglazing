# Local SEO Strategy

## Google Business Profile

### Categories
- **Primary:** Glazier
- **Secondary:** Glass repair service, Window installation service, Glass & mirror shop (if showroom)
- Limit to 3-5 total. Push detail into Services list.

### Required setup
- Accurate NAP, hours, service area (all Melbourne suburbs)
- Enable messaging / quote requests
- Set attributes: on-site services, online estimates, emergency service (if 24/7)
- Seed Q&A with top retrofit/pricing questions
- 20-40 photos month one, grow to 80-150+ (real project photos by suburb)

### Review targets
- 0-6 months: 40-60 reviews, 4.7+ average
- 6-18 months: 120-200+ reviews, steady velocity (1+/week)
- Request reviews same-day via SMS/email with direct Google review link
- Prompt for suburb + service type detail in reviews
- Respond to every review

## Citations (priority order)

### Tier 1 — Core platforms
Google Business Profile, Apple Maps (Business Connect), Bing Places, Facebook Business Page, LinkedIn Company Page

### Tier 2 — Major AU directories
Yellow Pages Online, True Local, Hotfrog, LocalSearch, White Pages, PureLocal, StartLocal, AussieWeb, dLook, Local.com.au

### Tier 3 — Review platforms
Word of Mouth (WOMO), ProductReview.com.au, Yelp

### Tier 4 — Trade-specific
AGWA (Australian Glass and Window Association), AGGA, Hipages, Synap Glazier directory, Trade Heroes

### Tier 5 — Melbourne-specific
melbournebd.com.au, melbourneservices.com.au, North East Directory

**NAP must be identical across all listings.**

## Link building

### High-yield tactics
1. **AGWA/AGGA membership** — member directory link + credibility
2. **Local sports club sponsorships** — junior football/netball/cricket in key suburbs (Burwood, Camberwell, Hawthorn). Negotiate website listing with follow link.
3. **Guest posts** — Melbourne home improvement blogs: MelbournesBuilder.com.au, Oneflare Blog, HomeownersAssociation.com.au
4. **Co-authored case studies** — with architects/builders on retrofit projects
5. **Local chamber memberships** — member directory links
6. **Community groups** — energy/sustainability groups with sponsor pages

### Content for link acquisition
- Retrofit cost guide (annually updated)
- "How double glazing boosts Melbourne homes' energy star ratings"
- "Noise-reduction retrofits near busy tram lines"
- "Retrofit vs full replacement in period terraces"

### Forums (reputation, not link farming)
- r/AusRenovation
- Homeone.com.au
- Houzz AU

## Priority suburb pages (Phase 10)

Batch 1: Burwood, Camberwell, Glen Waverley, Hawthorn, Box Hill
Then batches of 3-5 expanding outward.

Each suburb page needs: unique intro copy, local landmarks, suburb-specific testimonials, areaServed schema.
