## Energy efficiency / thermal keywords

Manufacturers and energy-focused window companies emphasise how much heat is lost/gained via glass: up to about 40% of heating energy lost and up to 87% of summer heat gain can be via windows in typical Australian homes. That supports strong content around Low‑E, argon-filled IGUs and retrofit vs replacement.magnetite+1

## How to use this for site architecture

Rather than try to build a separate page for every single phrase and question variation, cluster them into:

- 3–4 core commercial landing pages
    - Soundproof / acoustic windows Melbourne
    - Energy efficient / Low‑E double glazing Melbourne
    - Retrofit vs replacement / window types comparison
    - Heritage & period homes glazing solutions
- Supporting product/technical pages
    - Acoustic laminated glass + PVB / premium noise package (you can feature your ~37 dB Rw acoustic PVB + Low‑E unit and relate it to broader acoustic glass ranges discussed in trade content).quickfitglass+1
    - Low‑E glass, argon gas, U‑values, condensation fixes (each can be a sub‑page or major section).
- Long‑form guides & ROI content
    - “How to reduce traffic noise in a Melbourne house”
    - “Is double glazing worth it in Melbourne? Payback, energy savings and comfort”
    - “Secondary glazing vs double glazing vs full replacement for Melbourne homes”

Populate each with the relevant People Also Ask questions as H2/H3 FAQ sections and structure key comparison queries in tables and lists so they are snippet‑ready.
